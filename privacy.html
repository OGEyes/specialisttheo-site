Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Specialist Theo ("we," "us," or "our") operates an AI-powered dispatch and missed-call text-back service for small businesses ("Clients"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information from both our Clients and the end-users ("Customers") who interact with our service via phone calls and text messages.

1. Information We Collect

From Clients (business owners):

  • Business name, address, and contact information
  • Owner name, email, and phone number
  • Business operational details (hours, services, pricing)
  • Payment information (processed via Stripe; we do not store card details)

From Customers (end-users):

  • Phone number from which a call or text is placed
  • Date, time, and duration of calls
  • Content of text messages exchanged with our AI dispatcher (Theo)
  • Information voluntarily shared during the conversation (name, address, service requested)

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the dispatch and booking service that our Client has subscribed to
  • To respond to missed calls on behalf of the Client
  • To schedule service appointments
  • To send confirmation and follow-up messages
  • To improve our AI models and service quality (in aggregated, anonymized form)
  • To comply with legal obligations and carrier requirements

3. Information Sharing

We share customer conversation data only with the Client whose business is being represented. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We use the following service providers to operate our platform:

  • Twilio — for telephony and SMS delivery
  • Anthropic — for AI conversational processing
  • Airtable — for data storage
  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Vercel — for website hosting

Each of these providers operates under their own privacy policies and security standards.

4. SMS / Text Message Consent

When a Customer calls a business that uses Specialist Theo and the call is missed, the Customer's phone number is used to send an automated SMS response. This is considered consent under industry standards because the Customer initiated the contact by calling first.

Customers may opt out of further messages at any time by replying STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, or QUIT to any message. Standard message and data rates may apply.

Reply HELP at any time to receive support information.

5. Data Retention

We retain conversation and call data for as long as the Client account is active. Upon Client account termination, data is retained for 90 days for billing and dispute resolution purposes, then deleted.

Customers may request deletion of their information at any time by emailing the address below.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure API tokens, and access-controlled databases. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of data in transit or storage.

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is intended for adult business operators and their adult customers. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 13.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access information we have about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of future communications
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
